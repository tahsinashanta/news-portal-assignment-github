Frequently Asked Questions

Question-1: What is the difference among var-let-const?

Answer: let/const cannot be redeclared, var can be redeclared and reassigned. Let can be reassigned. var can be declared without initialization. let/const cannot be declared without initialization.

Question-2: What is the difference among arrow and regular function?

Answer: Arrow functions are only callable and not constructible. Regular functions created using function declarations or expressions are constructible and callable. Arrow functions have a shorter syntax and thus require less code.

Question-3: Why should the template string be used?

Answer: Template strings allows to use strings or embedded expressions in the form of a string. They are enclosed in backticks.
